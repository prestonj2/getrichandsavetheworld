How RO Systems Work (Simply)

Picture a series of increasingly fine filters:

  1. Pre-filters: remove sediment, rust, and chlorine
  2. RO membrane: a tight barrier that blocks dissolved solids and contaminants
  3. Post-carbon filter: polishes the taste before delivery

Most systems include a small storage tank and a dedicated faucet. The process wastes some water (typically 3:1 ratio), but for drinking and cooking use, the benefits far outweigh the cost.
